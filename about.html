Background of our company

At Ashmit Cloud, we are committed to transforming the way businesses operate by providing cutting-edge cloud services and IT software solutions. With a passion for technology and innovation, we offer scalable, secure, and reliable cloud platforms to support businesses of all sizes and industries. Whether you're a startup looking to leverage the power of the cloud or an established enterprise seeking advanced IT solutions, we have the tools, expertise, and dedication to help you succeed

Ashmit Cloud was founded in 2018 with a vision to provide businesses with cutting-edge cloud services and IT software solutions. From the beginning, we set out to make cloud technology accessible, scalable, and reliable for organizations of all sizes

With a global outlook, Ashmit Cloud initially established its headquarters in the United Kingdom and expanded its operations to India, with offices in Indore and Bhopal. Our international presence allows us to serve clients across multiple regions, bringing a unique blend of global expertise and local insights.

At Ashmit Cloud, we provide a variety of powerful cloud solutions to meet the unique needs of businesses across the globe. Whether you're looking to scale quickly, enhance your infrastructure, or streamline your operations, we offer the best cloud services from the top cloud providers: Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP)

AWS is a leading cloud platform that offers a wide range of infrastructure and application services. With AWS, businesses can run applications, store data, and manage networks with high scalability, reliability, and security.

At Ashmit Cloud, we take pride in offering robust, high-performance networks that span both the government and private sectors. With a strong infrastructure in place, we ensure seamless connectivity and reliable service delivery across industries, regions, and organizations

We understand the critical need for security, compliance, and high availability in the government sector. Our networks are designed to meet the stringent requirements of government agencies and public institutions, ensuring that sensitive data is managed securely and reliably.
